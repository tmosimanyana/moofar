About Moofar

Botswana's Premier Landscaping Experts

Since our founding, Moofar Proprietary Limited has been transforming outdoor spaces across Botswana. Led by Director Mooketsi Mapugwa and Manager Farai Madorobo, our team brings unmatched expertise in sustainable landscaping.

We understand Botswana's unique climate challenges and opportunities, creating landscapes that thrive year-round while conserving precious water resources.

  • Climate-Adapted Plant Selection
  • Water-Wise Irrigation Systems
  • Sustainable Maintenance Programs
  • Expert Design & Installation
