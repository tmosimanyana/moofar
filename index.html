Who We Are

Moofar (PTY) LTD is a Botswana-based, multi-disciplinary company dedicated to providing a wide range of innovative, reliable, and high-quality services. Established with a commitment to integrity, professionalism, and customer satisfaction, Moofar aims to be a trusted partner for individuals, organizations, and public entities across the country.

Mission

To deliver outstanding services through operational excellence, skilled workmanship, innovation, and a commitment to improving the communities and industries we serve.

Vision

To become a leading and trusted provider of diversified, sustainable, and customer-focused solutions in Botswana and the region.

Core Values

  • Integrity — Honesty & fairness.
  • Innovation — New solutions.
  • Excellence — Pursuing our best.
  • Customer Focus — Exceeding expectations.
