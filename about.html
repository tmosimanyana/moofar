About Moofar

Your Trusted Multi-Disciplinary Partner in Botswana

Who We Are

Moofar (PTY) LTD is a Botswana-based, multi-disciplinary company dedicated to providing a wide range of innovative, reliable, and high-quality services. Established with a commitment to integrity, professionalism, and customer satisfaction, Moofar aims to be a trusted partner for individuals, organizations, and public entities across the country.

Our diverse service offerings allow us to meet the needs of various sectors, ensuring efficiency, sustainability, and exceptional value. From landscaping and construction to logistics and consultancy, we bring expertise and dedication to every project we undertake.

Meet Our Leadership Team

Visionary co-founders driving sustainable development and service excellence

Moofar (PTY) LTD was established by two visionary co-founders who share a common passion for sustainable development, service excellence, and community empowerment. Together, they bring a powerful blend of leadership, expertise, and commitment that drives the company's mission forward.

MM

Mooketsi Mapugwa

Co-Founder & Director

With extensive experience in landscaping and business management, Mooketsi leads Moofar's strategic vision and ensures the highest standards of service delivery across all projects. His leadership is rooted in integrity, innovation, and a hands-on approach to building a company that not only delivers high-quality services but also creates employment opportunities and empowers youth.

FM

Farai Madorobo

Co-Founder & Manager

Farai oversees daily operations and project management, bringing expertise in horticulture and team coordination to ensure every client receives exceptional results on time and within budget. His commitment to operational excellence and community support ensures Moofar operates with a clear vision and dedication to measurable outcomes.

Future Outlook

Moofar aims to grow into one of Botswana's most reliable multi-service companies by expanding our operations, strengthening partnerships, and adopting innovative technologies. Our goal is to provide unmatched service excellence across all sectors we operate in, while continuing to support environmental sustainability and community development.
