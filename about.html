Our Journey in Francistown

The Moofar Story

Founded in September 2025, Moofar Proprietary Limited has quickly established itself as Francistown's premier landscaping service provider. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision: to beautify Botswana, one garden at a time.

Under the leadership of Director Mooketsi Mapugwa and Manager Farai Madorobo, we've built a reputation for excellence, innovation, and unwavering commitment to client satisfaction throughout Francistown and surrounding areas in Botswana.

We understand Francistown's unique climate, soil conditions, and terrain. This local expertise allows us to deliver landscaping solutions that are not only visually stunning but also sustainable and perfectly adapted to Botswana's environment. From drought-resistant plants to water-efficient irrigation systems, every aspect of our work reflects our deep knowledge of the region.
