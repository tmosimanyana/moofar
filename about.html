About Moofar

Who We Are

Moofar Proprietary Limited is a professional landscaping and horticulture service provider dedicated to transforming outdoor spaces in Francistown, Botswana. With years of experience in garden design, maintenance, and hardscaping, we pride ourselves on delivering quality work that exceeds our clients' expectations.

Our Mission

To create beautiful, functional, and sustainable outdoor spaces that enhance the quality of life for our clients while respecting the local environment and climate.

Why Choose Us?

  • Professional expertise in landscaping design and installation
  • Customized solutions tailored to your specific needs
  • Knowledge of local climate and plant varieties
  • Reliable and timely service
  • Competitive pricing and transparent communication
