About Moofar

Our Story & Vision

Founded in September 2025, Moofar Proprietary Limited is Botswana's trusted partner for landscaping, horticulture, and land maintenance. We are committed to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction in every project.

Our team of professional horticulturists, landscapers, and craftsmen bring expertise, creativity, and dedication to transform outdoor spaces into works of art.

Our Mission

To deliver premium landscaping and horticultural solutions while respecting the natural environment and exceeding client expectations.

Our Values

  • Excellence in every project
  • Sustainability & environmental responsibility
  • Customer satisfaction & trust
  • Innovation & creativity
