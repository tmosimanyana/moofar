About Moofar

Excellence in landscaping and horticulture since our founding

Our Story

Moofar Proprietary Limited is a leading landscaping and horticultural services company based in Francistown, Botswana. We specialize in creating and maintaining beautiful, sustainable outdoor spaces that thrive in Botswana's unique climate.

Under the leadership of Director Mooketsi Mapugwa and Manager Farai Madorobo, our team brings years of experience and local expertise to every project. We understand the challenges and opportunities of landscaping in Botswana, from water conservation to selecting plants that flourish in our environment.

Our Mission

To transform outdoor spaces across Botswana through sustainable landscaping practices, quality horticultural products, and exceptional customer service. We are committed to enhancing property values while respecting our natural environment.

Why Choose Moofar?

  • Local Expertise: Deep understanding of Botswana's climate and soil conditions
  • Sustainable Practices: Water-wise landscaping and native plant selection
  • Comprehensive Services: From design to maintenance, we handle it all
  • Quality Assurance: Using premium materials and proven techniques
  • Professional Team: Experienced, trained, and dedicated staff
