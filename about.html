About Moofar

Transforming Botswana's outdoor spaces with sustainable landscaping solutions

Who We Are

Moofar Proprietary Limited is a premier landscaping and horticulture service provider based in Francistown, Botswana. We specialize in creating and maintaining beautiful, sustainable outdoor spaces that thrive in Botswana's unique climate.

Our team combines local knowledge with professional expertise to deliver exceptional results for residential and commercial properties across the North-East District and beyond.

Our Mission

To provide world-class landscaping, horticultural, and property maintenance services that enhance the beauty and value of outdoor spaces while promoting environmental sustainability and supporting local communities.
