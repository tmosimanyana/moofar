About Moofar

Dedicated to crafting green, sustainable spaces that reflect the natural beauty of Botswana.

Who We Are

Founded in Botswana, Moofar Pty Ltd is a leading provider of landscaping, horticultural, and land maintenance solutions. Our expertise spans residential, corporate, and public projects, with a strong emphasis on quality, environmental responsibility, and long-term client relationships.

We believe that every outdoor space — no matter its size — holds the potential to inspire, rejuvenate, and sustain. That’s why our team works hand-in-hand with clients to design and maintain environments that thrive year-round.

Our Mission

To deliver environmentally conscious landscaping and horticultural solutions that enhance property value, promote biodiversity, and inspire sustainable living across Botswana.

Our Vision

To be Botswana’s most trusted name in green infrastructure — a company recognized for innovation, integrity, and care for both people and the planet.

Our Core Values

Excellence

We deliver work of the highest quality, ensuring every project exceeds expectations.

Sustainability

We implement practices that conserve water, protect soil, and promote healthy ecosystems.

Integrity

We operate transparently and ethically in every client interaction and decision.

Community

We support local development and invest in skills that uplift our people and environment.

Commitment to Sustainability

Environmental care is at the heart of everything we do. Moofar integrates eco-friendly methods such as water-efficient irrigation, compost-based soil health programs, and indigenous plant selection to promote biodiversity and reduce maintenance costs.

Through continual training and innovation, we help clients meet modern sustainability goals while creating outdoor spaces that are both functional and beautiful.
